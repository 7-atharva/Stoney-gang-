Description of the most Populaire cites

MUMBAI

Mumbai, formerly Bombay, is on the west coast of India, overlooking the Arabian Sea. It's Maharashtra's capital and India's most populous city, with over 20 million residents. Historically, it's been a vital trading center, from ancient times to British colonial rule. Mumbai's strategic location made it a key port, attracting merchants worldwide.

GOA

Goa, spanning 3,702 square kilometers, is a coastal paradise nestled on the western coast of India, renowned for its pristine beaches and vibrant culture. It's the smallest state by area in India yet boasts a rich tapestry of history, heritage, and natural beauty.
